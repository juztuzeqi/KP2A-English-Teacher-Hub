📋 Monthly Assessment Plan 2026

Full Academic Year
📌 Grades 1–9 · MA 1 to MA 4 / mastery tests

Grade 1

Beginner
📘 MA 1
Preliminary Unit + Unit 1-3: About myself/school, Pronouns (I, He, She, We, They, You, It), To Be (Am, Is, Are)
📗 MA 2
Unit 4-7: This/That, A/An, Possessive Adjectives (My, His, Her, Your, Its)
📙 MA 3
Unit 8-9: Simple Present Tense, Present Continuous Tense
📕 MA 4
Unit 10-11: Can/Cannot, Prepositions (On, In, Under, Behind, Over, In front of)

Grade 2

Elementary
📘 MA 1
Unit 1: Noun, Singular & Plural Nouns, Countable & Uncountable. Unit 2: Article A, An, The, Quantifiers
📗 MA 2
Unit 2: Prepositions. Unit 3: Personal Pronouns (Subject & Object), Possessive Adjectives. Unit 4: Adjectives (overview)
📙 MA 3
Unit 5: Question Words. Unit 6: Present Continuous Tense
📕 MA 4
Unit 7: Simple Present Tense vs Present Continuous Tense. Unit 8: Simple Past Tense & Past Continuous Tense

Grade 3

Building
📘 MA 1
Unit 1-4: Nouns (Proper/Common, Singular/Plural, Countable/Uncountable). Unit 5: Personal Pronouns, Possessive Adjectives & Pronouns
📗 MA 2
Unit 6-7: Punctuation & Capital Letter. Unit 8-9: Prepositions & Articles. Unit 10: Question Words
📙 MA 3
Unit 11: Linking Verb. Unit 12-13: Simple Present & Simple Past Tense. Unit 14: Present Continuous Tense
📕 MA 4
Unit 15-19: Past Continuous & Simple Future Tense, Adjectives, Adverbs, Gender

Grade 4

Intermediate
📘 MA 1
Unit 1: Nouns (Review: Common/Proper, Singular/Plural, Countable/Uncountable). Unit 2-3: Articles & Collective Nouns
📗 MA 2
Unit 4-8: Personal Pronouns, Verb Agreement, Linking Verb; Prepositions; Statements & Questions, Positive/Negative Sentences
📙 MA 3
Unit 9-14: Question Words, Why & Because, Review Do/Did; Short Answers & Replies; Simple Present & Present Continuous Tense
📕 MA 4
Unit 15-22: Simple Past & Past Continuous; Present Perfect & Simple Future; Conjunctions, Adjectives (Comparative/Superlative); Can/Could; Punctuation

Grade 5

Upper Primary
📘 MA 1
Cluster 1: Noun (a–k all subtopics)
📗 MA 2
Cluster 2: Verb (a–e all subtopics). Cluster 3.a-c: Simple Present Tense, Present Continuous Tense, Form of Verbs
📙 MA 3
Cluster 3.d-i: Irregular Verbs, Simple Past, Past Continuous, Present Perfect, Past Perfect Tense
📕 MA 4
Cluster 3.j-k: Simple Future Tense, "Going to" form. Cluster 4-5: Grammar Exercise 1 & 2 (comprehensive review)

Grade 6

Pre-Secondary
📘 MA 1
Unit 1-4: Punctuation, Tense Sequence, Modals, Adjectives. Unit 5-6: Forming Adjectives, Comparison of Adjectives
📗 MA 2
Unit 7-10: Adverbs, Forming Adverbs, Comparison, Adverbials of Duration. Unit 11-12: Preposition, Prepositional Phrase & Phrasal Verb. Unit 13: Conditional Sentence Type 1
📙 MA 3
Unit 14-16: All Pronouns (Personal, Possessive, Reflexive, Relative, Indefinite, Interrogative, Demonstrative, Reciprocal). Unit 17-19: Conjunctions & Sentence Connectors
📕 MA 4
Unit 20-25: Types of Sentences, Sentence Structure, WH- Questions, Positive/Negative Statements & Questions, Question Tags. Unit 26-29: Direct/Indirect Speech, Indirect Questions, Transitive/Intransitive Verbs, Active/Passive Voice

Grade 7

Junior Secondary
📘 MA 1
Unit 1-12: Nouns & Possession (Common/Proper, Countable/Uncountable, Singular/Plural, Quantifiers, Articles, Some/Any, Collective Nouns, Forming Nouns, Noun Phrases, Possession, Gender).
📗 MA 2
Unit 13-17: Verb Fundamentals (Verb Agreement, Forming Verbs, Forms of Verb, Verbs 'to be', 'to have', 'to do').
📙 MA 3
Unit 18-23: Tenses Fundamentals: Present & Past (Simple Present, Present Continuous, Present Perfect, Simple Past, Past Continuous, Past Perfect).
📕 MA 4
Unit 24-27: Tenses Fundamentals: Future & Synthesis (Simple Future, Going to, Infinitives, Synthesis and Transformation Skills).

Grade 8

Intermediate Plus
📘 MA 1
Unit 1-4: Prepositions & Intro to Adjectives (Preposition, More Prepositions, Prepositional Phrases/Phrasal Verbs, Adjectives)
📗 MA 2
Unit 5-7: Adjectives Deep Dive (Forming Adjectives, Comparison, Adjectival Phrases)
📙 MA 3
Unit 8-12: Adverbs & Modals (Adverbs, Forming Adverbs, Comparison, Adverbial Phrases, Adverbials of Duration)
📕 MA 4
Unit 13-16: Connectors & Sentence Types (Modals, Conjunctions 1&2, Sentence Connectors) MID-COURSE MASTERY TEST
📖 MA 5
Unit 17-21: Sentence Crafting & Questions (Types of Sentences, Sentence Structure, Positive/Negative Sentences & Responses, Question Tags)
📖 MA 6
Unit 22-23: Verbal Forms & Voice (Gerund & Infinitive, Active & Passive Voice)
📖 MA 7
Unit 24-27: Reported Speech & Transitivity (Direct/Indirect Statements, Indirect Questions, Transformation of Speech, Transitive/Intransitive Verbs)
🏆 MA 8
Unit 28-30: Synthesis & Conditionals (Linking Messages/Combining Sentences, Synthesis & Transformation Skills, Conditional Sentences) FINAL LEVEL-UP TEST

Grade 9

Advanced
📘 MA 1
Unit 1 → Review of Nouns & Possession (all subtopics)
📗 MA 2
Unit 2–3: Review of Verbs & Quantifiers + Be/Have/Do exercises
📙 MA 3
Unit 4-7: Review of Verbs & Quantifiers (continued) + Be/Have/Do exercises (detailed reinforcement)
📕 MA 4
Unit 8-13: Tenses Review (Present & Past) + Stative Verbs, Present Perfect Progressive MID-COURSE MASTERY TEST
📖 MA 5
Unit 14-20: Tenses Lanjutan & Future (Past Perfect Progressive, All Future Tenses, Going to & other futures)
📖 MA 6
Unit 21-22: Conditionals, Wishes, & Modals Lanjutan
📖 MA 7
Unit 23-27: Competency Test, Comparison, Prepositions Review Lanjutan
🏆 MA 8
Unit 28-33: Pronouns, Conjunctions, Speech Review, and Bonus (Idioms, Phrasal Verbs) FINAL LEVEL-UP TEST
